The process

Three steps.
One goal: informed choice.

Step 01

We collect

We monitor trusted news outlets, official artist statements, open letters, and confirmed press reports from across the internet — and extract publicly available information about each artist's cultural stance.

Guardian · NME · Rolling Stone · BBC · Billboard
Step 02

We map

Every entry is verified, sourced, and classified by the strength of the artist's position — not our opinion of it. We link directly to the original source so you can read it yourself and verify everything independently.

Critical · High · Low — classified by evidence
Step 03

You decide

We surface that transparency at the exact moment it matters — as a live alert in your browser when you're buying tickets, or instantly accessible on mobile when you're deciding whether to stream, share, or show up.

Privacy

We know nothing about you

ArtSiren is a transparency tool — not a surveillance one. We apply that same principle to how we treat you.

No data collected

The extension runs entirely inside your browser. Nothing is ever transmitted — we never know which pages you visit or which artists you search.

No account required

No sign-up, no email, no profile. You are completely anonymous — on the website, in the app, and in the extension.

Open to audit

Every source is public and linked directly in the app. You don't have to trust us — you can verify every single entry yourself.
