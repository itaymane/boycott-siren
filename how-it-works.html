The process

Three steps. One goal.

Every piece of information you see in ArtSiren went through the same pipeline — collect, verify, deliver.

Step 01

We collect

We monitor trusted outlets — the Guardian, BBC, Rolling Stone, NME, Billboard, and more — and gather publicly available information about artists' cultural stances. Only what is said or done in public.

Public actions only · Named sources
Step 02

We verify & map

Every entry is linked to the original source — a named journalist, an official statement, or a confirmed press report. We classify by the strength of the evidence, not our interpretation of it.

Classified by evidence · Linked to source
Step 03

You decide

We surface that context the moment it matters — as a live alert in your browser while buying tickets, or instantly on your phone. The information is yours. What you do with it is entirely your call.

Browser alert · Mobile search · Your call

Privacy

We know nothing about you

ArtSiren is a transparency tool — not a surveillance one. We apply that same principle to how we treat you.

No data collected

The extension runs entirely inside your browser. Nothing is ever transmitted — we never know which pages you visit or which artists you look up.

No account required

No sign-up, no email, no profile. You are completely anonymous — on the website, in the app, and in the extension.

Open to audit

Every source is public and linked directly. You don't have to trust us — you can verify every single entry yourself, independently.
