About Boycott Siren

Our Mission

Boycott Siren provides transparent, verified information about artists who have publicly taken positions on boycotting Israel. We believe consumers deserve to know this information when making entertainment choices.

Our Approach

All information in our database comes from publicly documented sources including:

  • Official BDS movement documentation (archived)
  • Artists for Palestine UK statements
  • Wikipedia and major news outlets
  • Official artist statements and announcements

Verification Process

Every artist in our database has been verified through multiple sources. We provide direct citations so you can independently verify all information.

Privacy & Ethics

We collect zero personal data. The extension runs entirely locally in your browser. We link to archived versions of boycott organization websites to document information while avoiding direct traffic to these sites.
