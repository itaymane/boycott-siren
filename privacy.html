Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Privacy First: This extension operates entirely locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal information or browsing activity.

What We Don't Collect

This extension does not collect:

  • Your browsing history
  • Personal information
  • Search queries
  • Purchase history
  • Location data
  • IP addresses
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely within your browser:

  • Artist information is stored locally in the extension files
  • Detection happens in real-time on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No network requests are made by the extension

Permissions Explained

activeTab: Allows the extension to see the current page when you click the extension icon. This is standard for browser extensions.

Host permissions (ticketmaster.com, etc): Allows the extension to detect artist names on these specific websites. The extension only activates on sites you explicitly visit.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Storage

Any preferences you set (if applicable in future versions) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.

Updates to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
